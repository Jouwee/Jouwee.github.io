Emergent RPG

This is a game prototype I'm working since november of 2024, inspired by games such as Dwarf Fortress, Caves of Qud, and Rimworld.

The goal of this project is to create a turn-based RPG with an emergent storyline.

Download current version - 0.0.7 (2.5 MB)

Current features

  • Procedurally generated world with 3 biomes
  • Family-lines for up to 750 years of history (see history.log)
  • 3 different enemies that would cause conflicts during history
  • Artifacts generated through history
  • Ability to navigate to any world tile and talk/fight people
  • Talk to people to figure out the history of the world
  • Codex of all information collected
  • Procedurally generated weapons for you to use
  • Discover the history and collect powerful artifacts

Contact me via Github on https://github.com/Jouwee/history-generator-rpg

Changelog

Version 0.0.7 (2.5 MB) (2024-12-22)

This version was all about creating an inventory and adding ways for you to collect weapons and artifacts.
  • [new] Three weapon types: Sword, Mace and Lance
  • [new] Spawn random weapons at ruined settlements
  • [new] People with artifacts will use them in battle
  • [new] After killing someone with an item, they drop the item
  • [new] Inventory panel for equiping items

Version 0.0.6 (2.5 MB) (2024-12-18)

This version was all about generating artifacts through history, and adding the ablity of you to investigate and find information about them
  • [new] Different species: Humans, Fiends, Leshens
  • [new] Random chance of spawning Fiends and Leshens as great beasts through history
  • [new] Great beasts attack nearby settlements randomly
  • [new] You can visit and fight alive great beasts, shown as caves in the map
  • [new] When someone kills a great beast, it can create and artifact form it's corpse
  • [new] Artifact inheritance upon death
  • [new] More detailed battle resolution (see history.log)
  • [new] New knowledge codex: Collect and see information about people, creatures, artifacts and places
  • [new] New talk interaction dialog
  • [new] You can ask people about who they are, adding info to your codex
  • [new] You can ask people rumors they heard, adding info to your codex
  • [new] FPS counter on F3
  • [bug] Non-remarkable people ended up being immortal

This is the first version I'm releasing of the game. I had some prior versions to this, but it was more experiments than anything.
  • [new] Procedurally generated world with 3 biomes
  • [new] Family-lines for up to 500 years of history (see history.log)
